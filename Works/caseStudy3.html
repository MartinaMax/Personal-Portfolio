FIGMA PROTOTYPE

Terra Teas is an e-commerce platform where the goal is not only to deliver to the customer wide choice of Asian teas but also the feeling and experience of its origin. The webshop is focused on presenting to the user where the tea originated and how to prepare it with the traditional brewing in mind.

Weaving together content, intuitive mobile patterns, interaction design and information architecture we aimed to create a tea lover heaven. From the moment customers lands on the site, they are transported to the origins of each tea, guided by storytelling and imagery that captured the essence of culture.
